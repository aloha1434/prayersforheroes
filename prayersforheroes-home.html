To all of the heroes that fight so valiantly to safeguard our lives and our freedom.
Each week, I will post a new prayer for a certain group. Some of these prayers will be general, rotating through police, military, fire, FBI. Others will be more specific or individualized.
Anyone is welcome to post prayers of their own below as well as encouraging messages.
You are also free to pray for specific individuals you know serving the American people.
God bless.
This week's prayer:
This week Lord, we ask that You watch over the U.S. Marine Corps.
Bless them for their courage and determination. Guard and guide them.
Thank You, Jesus. Amen.

General Prayers

Lord Jesus, I ask that You meet every service member where they are right now.
Uplift them; bring them strength and courage. Remind them they are loved. Amen.

Thank You, God, for every man and woman willing to risk their lives for the safety of the American people.
Bless them, and remind them they are loved. Watch over them this week. In Jesus' Name.

I thank You, Jesus, for all the men and women who have offered their lives in return for our freedom.
Bless them, and their families. Amen.

Posting rules:
Prayers/messages can be for a group or for individuals.
If you pray over a specific individual, no last names may be included.
No crude language or references.
Be respectful, and don't reveal private details.
Include your email and name, but be assured these will never be shown onsite.
If you wish to allow your name to show, sign only your first name to the end of your prayer/message.
Always note whether your prayer is general, or for a specific group.
Thanks!

P.S. If you'd like your prayer to be used as one of the weekly prayers, either email or include that as a note at the bottom of your message in the submitted form.
