This week's prayer:
This week Lord, we ask that You watch over the U.S. Marine Corps.
Bless them for their courage and determination. Guard and guide them.
Thank You, Jesus. Amen.

Military

Thank You, Lord, for the members of the U.S. military.
We are so thankful for their bravery, and their hard work to protect the citizens of this nation.
Bless them, Lord Jesus. Draw them ever closer to You. Remind them of Your love.
Guard their minds and their hearts.

Dear Abba, please safeguard our courageous military as they fight to protect us in this conflict with Iran.
Give them peace, wisdom, hope, and blessings. Protect them, and bring swift healing to those who need it.
Thank You for these amazing men and women. Help them know they are loved.
In Jesus' Name, Amen.

Army

Lord God watch over the Army's Golden Knights. Protect them in each flight, each jump, each mission.
Keep them safe; give them peace, hope, and joy each day. Amen.

Airborne

Lord, take care of the Airborne teams this week. I ask You bless them, and keep them. Watch over them. Take care of their families. Grant them peace and hope. In Your Name.

Rangers

Lord, take care of the Rangers this week. Please guide them, both in training and combat. Give them joy, and clarify their purpose. And grant peace to all remembering Black Hawk Down this week. In Jesus' Name.

SF-Green Berets

Lord, watch over the Green Berets this week as they serve you and the people of this nation. Guide them, grant them wisdom, and be their security. Uphold them with Your righteous right hand. In Jesus' Mighty Name.

Delta Force

Lord Jesus, watch over the Delta Force. Guard them. Help them to be prosperous in their life-saving endeavors. Thank You for this amazing team, and for their not-so-standard way of life. Amen.

(SOAR) Night Stalkers

Thank You, Jesus, for the brave Night Stalkers! Bless them, and guard them on each and every op. Give them wisdom, and grant them peaceful confidence. Thank You, Lord. Amen.

Air Force

Lord, please watch over the brave pilots and fearless mechanics of the Air Force this week. Give them wisdom and confidence, grace and peace, hope and joy. Guide them in Your Light. In Jesus' Name, Amen.

Pararescue Jumpers (PJs)

Lord, watch over the Pararescue Jumpers this week. Protect them in training and in the field. Watch over them, and those they save and protect. We thank You for the brave PJs, and for all they sacrifice to get the military out alive. In Your Mighty Name.

Navy

Thank You, Father, for the brave individuals who make up this nation's Navy. Guide them each day. Lead them in Your Wisdom and Truth. Keep them safe. Amen.
