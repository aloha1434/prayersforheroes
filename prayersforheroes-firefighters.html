This week's prayer:
This week Lord, we ask that You watch over the U.S. Marine Corps.
Bless them for their courage and determination. Guard and guide them.
Thank You, Jesus. Amen.

Firefighters

Lord God, protect every firefighter as they rush into infernos to rescue people. Give them wisdom and bravery. Ears to hear the cries of the trapped. And grant them peace in the aftermath. In Jesus' Name.

Dear Jesus, please watch over the courageous firefighters serving each and every community of the U.S. Give them strength, calm, and wisdom. Guard their hearts and their minds. In Jesus' Name, amen.
