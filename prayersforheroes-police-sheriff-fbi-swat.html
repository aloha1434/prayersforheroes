This week's prayer:
This week Lord, we ask that You watch over the U.S. Marine Corps.
Bless them for their courage and determination. Guard and guide them.
Thank You, Jesus. Amen.

Police

Lord, I ask that You take care of the policemen in Scottsboro, AL. Bring healing to the men, peace to them and their families. ~Elyza

Lord, take care of the York County Police Force. Please comfort the families of Mark, Cody, and Isaiah. Bring them hope and peace, dear Jesus. Please heal fully the two officers who are recovering. Thank You for preserving their lives. Watch over these amazing police officers, and guide them in the days ahead. Draw the man that caused this to You, and protect the force as they continue to protect the city under their care. In Jesus' Name.

Thank You for the brave men and women of our police forces across the United States. Watch over them, grant them favor and courage. And bring them joy and time with their families. In Jesus' Name.

Sheriff's Department

Dear God, take care of the various sheriff's departments this week. From administration to the brave souls working the streets. Give them peace and hope. Give them strength and courage. Watch over them and protect them. Give their families peace and faith. In Jesus' Name.

Thank You, Lord, for the amazing sheriff's departments that serve every area of the U.S. Watch over them. Remind them they're loved. Give them Your peace. In Jesus' Name, Amen.

FBI

Lord God, I ask You take care of the FBI this week. Grant them Your wisdom, and Your protection. From those in the office to those manning the streets. Guard them. In Jesus' Name.

Thank You, Adonai, for the valiant members of the FBI. Grant them Your peace and protection. Give them wisdom, and lead them in integrity. In Your Name.

SWAT

I pray over every SWAT member this week, Lord. Give them strength. Uphold them with Your righteous right hand. Uplift each team, and help trust ot build. Thank You for each one. And I thank them for their courage.
